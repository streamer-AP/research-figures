# Figure Classes

## editable-diagram

Use for:

- architecture diagrams
- method pipelines
- system block diagrams
- patent figures

Preferred backend:

- `drawio`

## method-overview

Use for:

- conference-paper method figures
- multi-stage pipelines
- structured model summaries

Preferred backend:

- `drawio` when topology matters
- `banana` when visual communication matters more

## visual-abstract

Use for:

- concise whole-paper summaries

Preferred backend:

- `banana`

## teaser

Use for:

- striking first-page paper visuals

Preferred backend:

- `banana`

## system-concept

Use for:

- hardware-software concept figures
- UAV, robotics, or multi-agent scenes

Preferred backend:

- `banana`

## chart-or-plot

Use for:

- ablations
- metrics
- line plots
- bar charts

Preferred backend:

- `plot`

## patent-figure

Use for:

- black-and-white technical disclosure figures

Preferred backend:

- `drawio`

## hybrid-figure

Use for:

- structured figure plus image panel
- polished visual layer over strict topology

Preferred backend:

- `hybrid`
